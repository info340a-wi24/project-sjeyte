Harmony - Mental Wellness Platform

Problem/Data Domain

Many people struggle with mental health issues like anxiety and depression, especially during difficult times. The pandemic has exacerbated these issues, emphasizing the need for accessible mental health care.

Existing Attempts: Several mental health apps exist, offering services ranging from mood tracking to guided meditation. However, gaps in accessibility and personalization remain.

References: WHO: Mental Health Strengthening Our Response, National Institute of Mental Health: Topics

Web App Description

Harmony is a mental wellness platform tailored for anyone seeking support, with a focus on accessibility for minority groups. Our aim is to provide a space where users can easily find and engage with mental health resources, fostering a community that supports each individual's journey towards better mental health.

Users engage with Harmony through features like the Mood Tracker, for logging emotional states; Guided Meditation, for relaxation; Resource Directory, for educational content; and Personalized Recommendations, which suggest activities based on user preferences. These tools are designed to be interactive, allowing users to actively participate in their mental wellness journey by providing inputs, making selections, and receiving tailored content.

Harmony addresses the need for accessible mental health support by offering a platform where users can discover resources, connect with others, and receive personalized care. This approach empowers users to take steps towards improving their mental health in a supportive, engaging environment.

Proposed Application

Our web application, Harmony, aims to bridge the accessibility gap in mental health support by providing a comprehensive suite of tools and resources tailored to individual needs.

Users:

Our platform is designed for individuals seeking to improve their mental wellness through self-guided tools and resources.

Interactive Features:

  • Mood Tracker
  • Guided Meditation
  • Resource Directory
  • Personalized Recommendations

Data Source: User-generated data and reputable mental health resources will inform our content and recommendations.

Impact: By offering accessible, personalized mental health support, Harmony aims to make a significant difference in the lives of those struggling with mental health challenges.

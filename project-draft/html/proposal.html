Harmony - Mental Wellness Platform

Problem/Data Domain

Many people struggle with mental health issues like anxiety and depression, especially during difficult times. The pandemic has exacerbated these issues, emphasizing the need for accessible mental health care.

Existing Attempts: Several mental health apps exist, offering services ranging from mood tracking to guided meditation. However, gaps in accessibility and personalization remain.

References: WHO: Mental Health Strengthening Our Response, National Institute of Mental Health: Topics

Proposed Application

Our web application, Harmony, aims to bridge the accessibility gap in mental health support by providing a comprehensive suite of tools and resources tailored to individual needs.

Users:

Our platform is designed for individuals seeking to improve their mental wellness through self-guided tools and resources.

Interactive Features:

  • Mood Tracker
  • Guided Meditation
  • Resource Directory
  • Personalized Recommendations

Data Source: User-generated data and reputable mental health resources will inform our content and recommendations.

Impact: By offering accessible, personalized mental health support, Harmony aims to make a significant difference in the lives of those struggling with mental health challenges.
